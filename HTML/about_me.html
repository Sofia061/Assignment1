About Me

My background combines both IT and the arts, which gives me a creative yet structured approach to problem-solving. Over the past years, I have built two websites as freelance projects, strengthening my skills in HTML, CSS, Databases, and Python. I also hold four CERTIPORT certifications, which reflect my technical knowledge and ability to learn quickly. Beyond technology, I have pursued an artistic education at the School of Fine Arts "Alexei Sciusev" and graduated with the top grades. My hobbies, such as singing, dancing, and task management, have shaped me into a disciplined and versatile person. I am fluent in Romanian, English, and Russian, and I actively engage in volunteer activities, from environmental projects to community events. With this combination of technical, artistic, and social skills, I am motivated to grow as a professional, contribute to impactful projects, and take on challenges that allow me to keep developing both personally and professionally.
